Hemp Strategy 2020

Emily Gogol, PhD
March 10th, 2020

Workshop Overview


Please join Infinite Tree and the Jackson Soil & Water Conservation District to learn best practices for growing hemp in our region. We are here to answer your questions, and help you develop a comprehensive hemp strategy for 2020.
When: April 1st 1-2:30pm
Where: 89 Alder Street, Central Point

Workshop topics will include:
  • Best practices for hemp production with an emphasis on infrastructure and design
  • Integrated Pest Management
  • Managing your grow for soil productivity
We host workshops throughout the nursery season, please register in advance.
