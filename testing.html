Sending Your Samples is Easy!

Some would say the waiting is the hardest part. Sending samples however, is easy. Choose the plant you'd like to test, take a few leaves from throughout the plant, and place them in the sample bag. We already have pre-printed labels for you to name your sample as you like. Simply drop the sample bag in the white shipping bag, attach the prepaid shipping label and you are good to go!
